Object of the game:

One day in the morning a tribe of insects broke into the kingdom and locked the princess inside bars. The only person who can eliminate, attack and kill these insects is SUPER MARIO. Mario the hero heard the story of what happened in the kingdom and rushed to eliminate them so that the princess can get freedom. But there are many obstacles and difficulties that super mario can face... The main goal is to race through the insects, and to survive the main antagonist opposite and save the princess. The game is a side-scrolling platformer, the player moves from the left side of the screen to the right side in order to reach the freedom of princess at the end of each level.
